About Carolina Chip Fab

A student-driven team building end-to-end pipelines for microdevices—design, fabrication, and validation under one roof.

Microelectronics Microfluidics Photonics Neuromorphics Quantum Chips

We prototype quickly, collaborate with campus labs, and turn research ideas into fabricated devices. R.A.M. Engineering backs us with mentorship and cross-team support—learn more at ram-engineering.org.

Design

Industry-grade tools and playbooks for schematics, layout, and verification.

Fabrication

Rapid prototyping with in-house rigs plus access to partner facilities when needed.

Test & Iterate

Short feedback loops—build, measure, and refine to accelerate device readiness.
